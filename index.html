04 — Bio

About

DJ Maros is a Valencia-based DJ specialising in acid techno. Originally active in Budapest, he has performed at club nights and private events, developing a precise and fast-paced mixing style.

His sets are built around driving rhythms and 303-led structures — relentless, hypnotic, and engineered for peak-time floors and warehouse rooms alike.
